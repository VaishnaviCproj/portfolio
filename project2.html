Visualizing Stock Market Performance using Tableau

Created on Mar 15, 2025

View Dashboard

Skills:

  • Data Visualization: Tableau
  • Data Analysis & Exploration: Time Series Analysis, Trend Identification
  • Dashboard Design: Interactive Filters, User-Centered Visualization
  • Stock Market Data Interpretation: OHLC Charts, Moving Averages, Trading Volume Trends

Project Overview:

Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze stock price trends, market performance, and trading volume. The dashboard enables users to explore historical price movements, compare multiple stocks, and filter data dynamically by year and stock name to gain insights into market behavior.

Key Contributions:

  • Interactive Dashboard: Designed multiple visualizations, including OHLC charts, stock price trends, and trading volume trends.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Implemented interactive filters (Year and Stock Name) for enhanced user experience.
  • Optimized Layout: Ensured readability, clear axis labels, and informative legends for better interpretation.
  • Enhanced Data Storytelling: Refined the color scheme and improved visualization clarity.

Results and Impact:

  • Enabled easy comparison of stock performance over time.
  • Provided real-time filtering for identifying trends and trading patterns.
  • Improved data-driven decision-making through historical stock price insights.
  • Designed with accessibility and usability improvements for professional presentation.

Learnings and Takeaways:

  • Mastered Tableau features like dual-axis charts, calculated fields, and real-time data filtering.
  • Gained deeper insights into stock market trends and the impact of moving averages.
  • Enhanced dashboard storytelling for better engagement and usability.
  • Optimized dashboard layouts for clarity, interpretation, and professional presentation.
