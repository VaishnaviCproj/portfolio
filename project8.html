Toman Bike Shop - Bike Share Dashboard

Created on Mar 17, 2025

Skills:

  • Data Visualization & Analytics: Power BI, DAX, SQL
  • Business Intelligence: KPI Analysis, Revenue Forecasting, Seasonal Trends
  • Data Processing: SQL Queries, Data Cleaning, Data Transformation

Project Overview:

This Power BI Dashboard provides insights into bike share revenue, ridership trends, profitability, and rider demographics. It enables data-driven decision-making by visualizing key metrics, peak revenue hours, seasonal demand, and rider behavior.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed an interactive dashboard: Designed KPIs, revenue heatmaps, seasonal trends, and demographic insights.
  • Analyzed profitability trends: Identified peak revenue hours and seasonal variations to optimize business strategies.
  • Segmented customer base: Differentiated between casual and registered riders for targeted marketing.

Results and Impact:

  • Provided clear insights into revenue trends and business growth between 2021 and 2022.
  • Helped optimize pricing and marketing strategies by identifying high-revenue time slots.
  • Demonstrated the effectiveness of data visualization in improving operational decision-making.

Learnings and Takeaways:

  • Improved proficiency in Power BI dashboard creation, KPI tracking, and DAX functions.
  • Developed strategies to analyze customer behavior and optimize business operations.
  • Explored predictive analytics to forecast future revenue and customer trends.
